Brand Overview: Abqary (عبقري)

Abqary is an online educational platform specialized in teaching Mental Math to children aged 5 to 13 years. It goes beyond traditional mathematics tutoring by using a gamified, interactive curriculum designed to replace rote memorization with deep understanding and rapid critical thinking. The platform connects students with live instructors (led by Mr. Abdelrahman Mansour) and uses a hybrid model of live sessions, recorded content, and competitive games to build not just mathematical skills, but a child’s overall character and self-esteem.
+4

The voice of Abqary is Encouraging, Confident, and Empowering. It speaks directly to the parent's desire for their child's success and to the child's need for fun.

Brand Values
These are the core principles that guide every interaction on the platform:

Understanding Over Memorization (التفكير لا التلقين):
Abqary stands firmly against rote learning. The core value is to develop the child’s mental agility and ability to visualize numbers, rather than just memorizing formulas.
+1

Empowerment & Confidence (بناء الثقة):
The primary goal isn't just solving math problems; it is building a child's self-esteem. The brand believes that mastering mental math is a gateway to overcoming fear and building a "hero" personality.
+1

Gamification & Fun (المتعة):
Education must be engaging. The brand values "positive competition," "play," and "challenges" as essential tools for learning, ensuring the child never feels bored or pressured.
+2

Accessibility (في المتناول):
The brand aims to be accessible to the middle class, offering high value at an affordable price point compared to physical centers, making elite mental skills available to more families.
+1

Brand Characteristics (Personality)
